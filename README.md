This is a mod that adds **AI players** to your game. 
You can now _**chat**_ with **AI players** in the game. 
We plan to make AI truly become a **_player entity and play with you_** in **v4.0**. 
For any issues, please submit [issues](https://github.com/bulefire-dream/NeuraCraft/issues).
You can also visit our [GitHub](https://github.com/bulefire-dream/NeuraCraft) homepage.
